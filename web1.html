The Lamborghini Urus is a high-performance luxury SUV that combines the sporty characteristics of a supercar with the versatility of an SUV,The Urus has reshaped the luxury SUV segment by bringing supercar performance to a more practical vehicle type, appealing to those who desire speed and luxury in a package that can handle more than just the racetrack. 


 Here are some key points about the Urus:

            Engine: It features a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine. Depending on the variant,
             it can produce between 641 to 666 horsepower (in the Urus Performante) and up to 800 horsepower in the hybrid Urus SE.
            Performance: 
            Acceleration: The base model accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in about 3.6 seconds, 
            with the Performante doing it in 3.3 seconds. 
            The Urus SE, the plug-in hybrid version, achieves this in 3.4 seconds.
            Top Speed: The Urus has a top speed of around 190 mph, with the Performante slightly higher at 306 km/h (190 mph), 
            and the SE at 312 km/h (194 mph).
